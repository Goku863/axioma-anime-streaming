Curated anime.
One streaming home.

AnimeForYou brings fans a thoughtful library of series, films, and premieres with personalized playlists and seamless playback across devices.

AnimeForYou began as a love letter to viewers. A small team of fans and curators set out to build a place where every anime felt easy to find and easy to watch.

We focused on quality first: better subtitles, smart recommendations, and a library shaped by genre, mood, and release schedule. We launched with a handful of series, and the stream grew because people trusted us to keep it worth watching.

Today AnimeForYou is still run by people who watch every season, test every app, and ask whether a show feels fresh. We keep the service simple, keep the library curated, and keep the experience fast across mobile, desktop, and TV.

Our favorite moments are when fans discover a new favorite or a long-ignored classic that feels brand new again.

How we work

01

Slower kickoffs.

Most projects fail in the first three weeks. We spend longer than feels reasonable in discovery so the rest of the engagement isn't a series of corrections.

02

Small, senior teams.

Every project is staffed by people who could run it on their own. There is no junior layer translating senior intent.

03

One opinion in the room.

We arrive at meetings with a clear point of view. You don't have to choose from a deck of options that none of us actually like.

04

Write everything down.

Every kickoff, every critique, every close-out gets a written memo. The archive is how the studio learns.

05

Build for year five.

An identity that only works on launch day isn't an identity. We design systems that hold up after the launch noise dies down.

06

Say no often.

We turn down most enquiries. The fits we take on get the studio's full attention — that math only works one way.
